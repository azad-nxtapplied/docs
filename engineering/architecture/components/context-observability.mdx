<Note>
  **Cross-cutting** — not layered; ambient, riding with every request and spanning every tier and band. Part of the [NXT Core platform](/engineering/architecture/nxt-core).
</Note>

Two ambient concerns merged because both ride with every request.

## Context

`correlation_id` · `workspace_id` · `principal` threaded through every call. This is a **correctness-critical carrier**: it is the scope [Data Access](/engineering/architecture/components/data-access) and [Configuration & Secrets](/engineering/architecture/components/configuration-secrets) read for tenant isolation, which is exactly why lower tiers never need to call [Identity & Access](/engineering/architecture/components/identity-access). It **must survive process hops** (API → worker, across the event bus).

## Observability

Structured **logging, metrics, and traces** for system health — best-effort operational telemetry.

<Warning>
  This is **ops telemetry, not decision evidence.** The immutable, hash-chained record of _why_ an action happened lives in the **AI Auditor** (Record plane), not here.
</Warning>

## Dependencies

Observability is built on top of Context. Both are ambient and depend on nothing in the foundation tiers — they are read _by_ those tiers, not the other way around.
