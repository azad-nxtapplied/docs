<Note>
  **Cross-cutting** — ambient, riding with every request and spanning every tier and band. Part of the [NXT Core architecture](/engineering/architecture).
</Note>

Two ambient concerns grouped together because both ride with every request.

## Context

Carries the ambient request scope — `correlation_id`, `workspace_id`, and `principal` — through every call. This is the scope [Data Access](/engineering/architecture/components/data-access) and [Configuration & Secrets](/engineering/architecture/components/configuration-secrets) read to apply tenant isolation, so the lower tiers get their workspace from Context directly. It travels with the request across process hops (API → worker, and across the event bus).

## Observability

Provides structured **logging, metrics, and traces** for system health — the operational telemetry that tells you how the platform is running.

<Note>
  Observability covers **system health**. The immutable, hash-chained record of _why_ an action happened lives in the **AI Auditor** (Record plane), which keeps the two concerns cleanly separated.
</Note>

## Dependencies

Observability builds on Context. Both are ambient and read by the foundation tiers rather than depending on them.
